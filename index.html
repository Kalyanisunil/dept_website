B.Voc in Software Development
(Conducted by Department of Information Technology)
The vocational programme B.Voc. in Software Development is a judicious mix of skills, professional education related to Software Development and also appropriate content of general education.
It is designed with the objective of equipping the students to cope with the emerging trends and challenges in the Software Development environment. Duration of the course: The duration of B.Voc. Software Development course shall be three years consisting of six semesters. The duration of each semester shall be five months inclusive of the days of examinations. There shall be at least 90 working days in a semester and a minimum 540 hours of instruction in a semester.
